Burrito Bowls

Vegan burrito bowls! Healthy and nutritious! Can be easily made into tacos or nachos.

Ingredients

    1 yellow onion
    1 red onion
    2 bell peppers, any color will work
    1 can of black beans
    1 cup of corn
    oil for cooking
    seasonings: chili powder, cumin, paprika, cayenne powder
    salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Prep all of your veggies by dicing into bite sized pieces
  2. Heat oil in the pan and when hot, add the onions and seasonings of choice. Sautee for 4 minutes.
  3. Add the peppers and continue sauteeing until peppers are cooked.
  4. Add corn and black beans and cook until everything is hot
  5. Enjoy! Serve with brown rice, tortillas, or over nacho chips. Add your favorite salsa or guacamole for a kick!
