Spaghetti

Scrumptious spaghetti with a wonderful pasta sauce and lentils. You will be going back for seconds!

Ingredients

    1 package of whole wheat noodles
    1 can of Prego Spaghetti Sauce
    1 can of lentils
    4 or 5 leaves of fresh basil
    1 tsp garlic salt or to taste

Steps

  1. Fill a pot with water and and a pinch of salt. Turn your stove on medium and bring water to a boil. Place lid on pot to boil faster.
  2. Once water is boiling, add noodles and cook for the time on the box.
  3. Drain noodles with a strainer and add back to the pot. Poor in your pasta sauce and lentils. Mix.
  4. Optional: add fresh basil and garlic powder
  5. Enjoy!
